# Client Acquisition Plan (15 Days) - Turkey First

## Campaign Status (Updated: April 17, 2026)
- Start date: April 16, 2026 (Day 1)
- Current day: April 17, 2026 (Day 2 of 15)
- Current phase: Foundation (Days 1-3)
- Daily minimum: 5 new cold emails
- Total minimum by end of Day 2: 10 sent emails

## Goal
- Get the first paid project using cold email.
- Send at least 5 emails/day for 15 days (minimum 75 total emails).
- Focus on Turkey first, then expand to other countries.

## Best Target to Start: Agencies or SaaS?
- Best starting point: **Agencies first**, then SaaS.
- Why agencies first:
- They already sell services and can outsource implementation fast.
- They feel pain quickly (delivery pressure, automation gaps).
- Smaller contract value but faster first deal.
- Why add SaaS in parallel:
- Bigger upside and recurring work (sales ops, AI automation, CRM workflows).
- Usually slower sales cycle.

## Recommended Prospect Mix
- 60% Agencies (marketing agencies, lead-gen agencies, RevOps consultancies).
- 40% SaaS (B2B SaaS with active sales teams, 5-100 employees).

## Ideal Client Profile (Turkey)
- Team has 3+ sales reps or outbound activity.
- Uses CRM (HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, Zoho) or wants to.
- Clear pain:
- Slow follow-up.
- Messy CRM updates.
- No insight from calls/meetings.
- Can pay for a pilot project in 2-6 weeks.

## Offer Positioning (Keep It Simple)
- Primary offer: “I build practical AI sales automations that reduce manual work and improve follow-up speed.”
- Pilot offer examples:
- Voice notes to CRM updates.
- AI sales observer dashboard.
- Call/meeting intelligence summary + next actions.
- Pilot duration: 2-3 weeks.
- Pilot price approach:
- Fixed-price pilot (easy yes).
- Then monthly optimization/maintenance package.

## 15-Day Execution Plan

### Days 1-3: Build Pipeline Foundation
- Define 2 short offers (agency-focused + SaaS-focused).
- Prepare one-page portfolio links (your demo site + 1 short case-style PDF).
- Build lead list of 120 contacts:
- 70 agencies.
- 50 SaaS.
- Collect: name, role, company, email, LinkedIn, pain hypothesis.

### Days 4-15: Daily Outreach and Follow-up
- Send 5 new cold emails/day minimum.
- Also send follow-ups/day:
- Day +2 follow-up for non-replies.
- Day +5 second follow-up.
- Day +9 final follow-up.
- Daily target:
- 5 new emails.
- 5-10 follow-ups.
- End of each day:
- Log replies and objections.
- Improve next day messaging based on replies.

## Daily Workflow (90-120 min)
- 25 min: Find 5 qualified leads.
- 35 min: Personalize and send 5 cold emails.
- 20 min: Send follow-ups to older leads.
- 10 min: Update tracking sheet.
- 10-30 min: Reply quickly to positive responses.

## Cold Email Structure
- Subject line:
- “Quick idea for {{company}} sales follow-up”
- “Small AI workflow for {{company}} outbound team”
- Body format:
- 1 line personalized opener.
- 1 pain line.
- 1 concrete result line.
- 1 CTA line (15-minute call).

## Simple Template (Agency)
Hi {{first_name}},

I noticed {{agency_name}} is actively running outbound campaigns for clients.
Many teams lose leads because follow-ups and CRM updates stay manual.

I build lightweight AI sales workflows (voice notes to CRM, follow-up alerts, call insight summaries) so your team ships faster with fewer manual steps.

If useful, I can show a 15-minute demo tailored to one of your active client workflows.

Best,  
Reza

## Simple Template (SaaS)
Hi {{first_name}},

I saw {{company}} is growing its sales motion.
A common issue at this stage is slow follow-up and inconsistent CRM hygiene.

I help teams implement practical AI sales automations that improve response speed and pipeline visibility without changing the full stack.

Open to a short 15-minute call this week?

Best,  
Reza

## KPI Targets (15 Days)

- Positive reply target: 3%-6%.
- Discovery calls target: 4-8.

## Lead Sources (Practical)
- LinkedIn search (Turkey + role filters).
- Company websites (team/contact pages).
- Startup directories and local tech communities.
- Job boards (companies hiring sales/RevOps roles are good signals).

## Qualification Rules (Do Not Waste Time)
- Skip companies with unclear sales motion.
- Skip generic info@ emails if you can find decision maker emails.
- Prioritize roles:
- Founder, Head of Sales, Sales Manager, RevOps, Agency Owner.

## Rules for Better Results
- Personalize first 1-2 lines only (don’t over-write).
- Keep emails under 120 words.
- One CTA only.
- Follow up consistently; most replies come from follow-ups.
- Reply to positive responses in under 2 hours.
